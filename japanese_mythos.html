Yaoyorozu no Kami - Eight Million Gods

Enter the world of Shinto, where spirits dwell in every mountain, river, and tree. From the sun goddess Amaterasu to the mischievous yokai, discover the spiritual heart of Japan.

The Creation of Japan

In the beginning, there was chaos. From this void emerged the first gods: Ame-no-Minakanushi (Central Master of Heaven), followed by Takamimusubi and Kamimusubi. These kami created the islands of Japan.

Izanagi & Izanami

The divine couple who stood on the Floating Bridge of Heaven and stirred the primordial ocean with a jeweled spear. The drops that fell formed the islands of Japan.

Amaterasu

Born from Izanagi's left eye, she became the sun goddess and ancestor of the Imperial family. Her light illuminates the world.

When Izanami died giving birth to the fire god Kagutsuchi, Izanagi descended to Yomi (the underworld) to retrieve her. But he broke the taboo of looking at her decaying form, sealing the separation between life and death forever.
